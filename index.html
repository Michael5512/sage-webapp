Explain the mechanism of action of beta-blockers in simple terms
S

What are the major categories of mental disorders?
S
Great question! The major categories per DSM-5 are:

🔹 Neurodevelopmental — ADHD, Autism Spectrum
🔹 Psychotic Disorders — Schizophrenia
🔹 Mood Disorders — Depression, Bipolar
🔹 Anxiety Disorders — GAD, Panic Disorder
🔹 OCD & Related
🔹 Trauma-Related — PTSD
